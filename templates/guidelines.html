Recent Tab List Official Rules & Guidelines

This covers everything regarding the submission of verifications pertaining to the recent tab list

Level Submission Requirements

Core Requirements

  • Recent Tab Requirement: Levels must be found in the recent tab (there is no time duration regarding how recent they must be)
  • Verification Eligibility: You must complete at least 10 already existing levels on the recent tab list to be able to submit verifications. Once you have done so you will be given the Verifier role that will allow you to access the verification submissions channel in the Discord server.
    Note: Levels submitted before this rule's implementation (August 18, 2025) will still be placed
  • Level Backup: Be sure to make copies of verified levels in case a list level is suddenly deleted from the servers
  • Difficulty Assessment: When submitting, give your difficulty opinions in reference to the List and the in-game star rating system (e.g., "I think X is top 60 and a 6 star")

Level Restrictions

Not Allowed
  • Nerfed/Buffed Levels: Pre-existing rated levels that have been modified will not be accepted
  • Stolen Levels: Levels that copy pre-existing rated levels will not be accepted
  • Rate-Worthy Levels: If a level becomes rate-worthy or eventually gets rated, it is subject to rejection and/or removal from the list
  • Own Levels: The level CANNOT be your own level made for the list. It must be found in the recent tab and verified by someone else
  • Auto Levels: Auto levels will not be placed on the list
  • Platformers: Platformer levels will not be placed on the list
Special Cases
  • Partial Copying: Levels with specific parts taken from other levels are somewhat allowed, but the stolen section must not resemble the original in likeness
  • Human Input Required: For a level to be placed, it must require at least 1 human-made input to be beaten
  • Secret Ways: If a level has a secret way, you may alter the level to fix said secret way. You must check with a list mod to have your changes approved
  • Coins: Collecting coins are not required for placement
  • Filters: You may use in-game filters to filter your recent tab by length, song, etc.
Note: Speak with a list mod if you have any questions regarding partial copying or other special cases

Community Standards & Behavior

Expected Behavior
  • Respect: Treat all members with dignity and respect
  • Honesty: Always be truthful about your achievements
  • Sportsmanship: Accept defeats gracefully and celebrate others' wins
  • Constructive Feedback: Provide helpful criticism and suggestions
  • Help Others: Assist newcomers and share knowledge
  • Follow Rules: Adhere to all Discord and website guidelines
  • Report Issues: Alert staff to problems or violations
  • Stay Updated: Keep informed about rule changes
Prohibited Behavior
  • Harassment: Bullying, stalking, or intimidating others
  • Hate Speech: Discriminatory language based on race, gender, etc.
  • Spam: Excessive posting or self-promotion
  • Doxxing: Sharing personal information without consent
  • Impersonation: Pretending to be staff or other players
  • Drama: Creating or escalating conflicts unnecessarily
  • Inappropriate Content: NSFW or offensive material
  • Ban Evasion: Creating alts to bypass punishments

Verification Requirements

Video Submission Standards

All verification videos must meet these technical requirements to be accepted

Required Elements
  • Cheat Indicator: Must have MegaHack cheat indicator enabled (or any other mod that has a cheat indicator)
  • Audible Clicks: You must have audible clicks in your verification
  • Full Completion: Show the complete level from start to finish
  • Clear Video Quality: Video must be clear enough to verify legitimacy
Vanilla Players Exception: If you play vanilla Geometry Dash, the cheat indicator rule is an exception for you
Additional Notes
  • Handcam: Handcam is not required but it is good to have
  • Platform: YouTube links preferred for verification videos
  • Audio: Game audio must be audible throughout the video
  • Editing: Minimal editing allowed, no cuts during gameplay
Important: Make sure your verification clearly shows the level completion and meets all technical requirements

Level Submission Guidelines

Level Criteria

  • Difficulty: Must be Recent Tab difficulty (6-10 stars)
  • Quality: Well-decorated and engaging gameplay
  • Length: Appropriate length for difficulty
  • Originality: Unique and creative design
  • Verification: Must have a legitimate verification video
  • Accessibility: Level must be publicly available

Disqualifying Factors

  • Copied/Stolen: Levels that copy existing content
  • Inappropriate: Offensive or inappropriate content
  • Buggy: Levels with game-breaking bugs
  • Impossible: Levels that are humanly impossible
  • Auto/Spam: Auto levels or spam gameplay
  • Private: Levels that are not publicly accessible

Moderation & Appeals

Warning System

1st Offense: Warning + Record removal

2nd Offense: 7-day submission ban

3rd Offense: 30-day submission ban

Severe Violations: Permanent ban

Appeal Process

Step 1: Contact moderators on Discord

Step 2: Provide evidence/explanation

Step 3: Wait for review (1-7 days)

Step 4: Accept final decision

Best Practices

Quality over Quantity: Submit your best records

Patience: Reviews take time

Communication: Ask questions if unsure

Improvement: Learn from feedback
