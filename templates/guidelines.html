‚ùå Not Allowed
Nerfed/Buffed Levels: Pre-existing rated levels that have been modified will not be accepted

Auto Levels: Auto levels will not be placed on the list
Platformers: Platformer levels will not be placed on the list

Coins: Collecting coins are not required for placement
Filters: You may use in-game filters to filter your recent tab by length, song, etc.
Note: Speak with a list mod if you have any questions regarding partial copying or other special cases

Verification Requirements

Video Submission Standards

All verification videos must meet these technical requirements to be accepted

Required Elements
Cheat Indicator: Must have MegaHack cheat indicator enabled (or any other mod that has a cheat indicator)
Audible Clicks: You must have audible clicks in your verification
Full Completion: Show the complete level from start to finish
Clear Video Quality: Video must be clear enough to verify legitimacy
Vanilla Players Exception: If you play vanilla Geometry Dash, the cheat indicator rule is an exception for you
Additional Notes
Handcam: Handcam is not required but it is good to have
Platform: YouTube links preferred for verification videos
Audio: Game audio must be audible throughout the video
Editing: Minimal editing allowed, no cuts during gameplay
Important: Make sure your verification clearly shows the level completion and meets all technical requirements

Level Submission Guidelines

Level Criteria

Difficulty: Must be Recent Tab difficulty (6-10 stars)
Quality: Well-decorated and engaging gameplay
Length: Appropriate length for difficulty
Originality: Unique and creative design
Verification: Must have a legitimate verification video
Accessibility: Level must be publicly available

Disqualifying Factors

Copied/Stolen: Levels that copy existing content
Inappropriate: Offensive or inappropriate content
Buggy: Levels with game-breaking bugs
Impossible: Levels that are humanly impossible
Auto/Spam: Auto levels or spam gameplay
Private: Levels that are not publicly accessible

Moderation & Appeals

Warning System

1st Offense: Warning + Record removal

2nd Offense: 7-day submission ban

3rd Offense: 30-day submission ban

Severe Violations: Permanent ban

Appeal Process

Step 1: Contact moderators on Discord

Step 2: Provide evidence/explanation

Step 3: Wait for review (1-7 days)

Step 4: Accept final decision

Best Practices

Quality over Quantity: Submit your best records

Patience: Reviews take time

Communication: Ask questions if unsure

Improvement: Learn from feedback
