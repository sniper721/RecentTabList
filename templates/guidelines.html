ZERO TOLERANCE POLICY

HACKING & CHEATING - PERMANENT BAN

INSTANT PERMANENT BAN OFFENSES:

  • HACKING: Using any form of hacks, cheats, or exploits in Geometry Dash
  • SPEEDHACKING: Altering game speed to make levels easier
  • NOCLIP: Using noclip or similar collision bypasses
  • MACRO/BOT: Using automated clicking or macro programs
  • FRAME MANIPULATION: Altering frame data or physics
  • FAKE RECORDS: Submitting fabricated or edited completion videos
  • ACCOUNT SHARING: Letting others play on your account for records
  • MULTIPLE ACCOUNTS: Creating alternate accounts to bypass bans

CONSEQUENCES OF HACKING:

IMMEDIATE ACTIONS:
  • PERMANENT ACCOUNT BAN
  • IP ADDRESS BAN
  • ALL RECORDS DELETED
  • ALL POINTS RESET TO ZERO
  • DISCORD SERVER BAN
  • PUBLIC BLACKLIST
PREVENTION MEASURES:
  • DEVICE FINGERPRINTING
  • BROWSER TRACKING
  • AUTOMATIC ALT DETECTION
  • CROSS-PLATFORM REPORTING
  • COMMUNITY WATCHLIST
  • NO APPEALS ACCEPTED

DETECTION METHODS:

Our advanced detection systems monitor:

  • Frame Analysis: Automated frame-by-frame video analysis
  • Physics Validation: Checking for impossible movements or timings
  • Audio Analysis: Detecting altered audio or click patterns
  • Community Reports: Player-submitted evidence and reports
  • Cross-Reference: Comparing with known cheated attempts
  • Behavioral Patterns: Identifying suspicious submission patterns

Community Standards & Behavior

Expected Behavior
  • Respect: Treat all members with dignity and respect
  • Honesty: Always be truthful about your achievements
  • Sportsmanship: Accept defeats gracefully and celebrate others' wins
  • Constructive Feedback: Provide helpful criticism and suggestions
  • Help Others: Assist newcomers and share knowledge
  • Follow Rules: Adhere to all Discord and website guidelines
  • Report Issues: Alert staff to problems or violations
  • Stay Updated: Keep informed about rule changes
Prohibited Behavior
  • Harassment: Bullying, stalking, or intimidating others
  • Hate Speech: Discriminatory language based on race, gender, etc.
  • Spam: Excessive posting or self-promotion
  • Doxxing: Sharing personal information without consent
  • Impersonation: Pretending to be staff or other players
  • Drama: Creating or escalating conflicts unnecessarily
  • Inappropriate Content: NSFW or offensive material
  • Ban Evasion: Creating alts to bypass punishments

Record Submission Guidelines

Important Notice

All records must be legitimate and follow these strict guidelines. Fake or cheated records will result in immediate penalties.

Video Requirements

  • Platform: YouTube links only (youtube.com or youtu.be)
  • Quality: Minimum 720p resolution recommended
  • Audio: Game audio must be audible
  • Length: Show full attempt from start to completion/death
  • Visibility: Video must be public or unlisted (not private)
  • Editing: Minimal editing allowed, no cuts during gameplay

Technical Requirements

  • FPS: Show FPS counter if playing on high refresh rate
  • Clicks: Click sounds or visual indicators recommended
  • Progress: Must show clear progress percentage
  • Attempts: Show multiple attempts for credibility
  • Raw Footage: Unedited gameplay preferred
  • Handcam: Required for top 10 completions
  • Cheat Indicator: Must be enabled for modded clients

Cheat Indicator Clarification

For Modded Clients (Geode, MegaHack, etc.): Cheat indicator MUST be enabled and visible in your recording.

For Vanilla Geometry Dash: Cheat indicator is NOT required since vanilla GD doesn't have this feature.

Level Submission Guidelines

Level Criteria

  • Difficulty: Must be Recent Tab difficulty (6-10 stars)
  • Quality: Well-decorated and engaging gameplay
  • Length: Appropriate length for difficulty
  • Originality: Unique and creative design
  • Verification: Must have a legitimate verification video
  • Accessibility: Level must be publicly available

Disqualifying Factors

  • Copied/Stolen: Levels that copy existing content
  • Inappropriate: Offensive or inappropriate content
  • Buggy: Levels with game-breaking bugs
  • Impossible: Levels that are humanly impossible
  • Auto/Spam: Auto levels or spam gameplay
  • Private: Levels that are not publicly accessible

Moderation & Appeals

Warning System

1st Offense: Warning + Record removal

2nd Offense: 7-day submission ban

3rd Offense: 30-day submission ban

Severe Violations: Permanent ban

Appeal Process

Step 1: Contact moderators on Discord

Step 2: Provide evidence/explanation

Step 3: Wait for review (1-7 days)

Step 4: Accept final decision

Best Practices

Quality over Quantity: Submit your best records

Patience: Reviews take time

Communication: Ask questions if unsure

Improvement: Learn from feedback
