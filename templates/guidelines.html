Important Notice

All records must be legitimate and follow these strict guidelines. Fake or cheated records will result in immediate penalties.

üìπ Video Requirements

  • Platform: YouTube links only (youtube.com or youtu.be)
  • Quality: Minimum 720p resolution recommended
  • Audio: Game audio must be audible
  • Length: Show full attempt from start to completion/death
  • Visibility: Video must be public or unlisted (not private)
  • Editing: Minimal editing allowed, no cuts during gameplay

‚ö†Ô∏è Technical Requirements

  • FPS: Show FPS counter if playing on high refresh rate
  • Clicks: Click sounds or visual indicators recommended
  • Progress: Must show clear progress percentage
  • Attempts: Show multiple attempts for credibility
  • Raw Footage: Unedited gameplay preferred
  • Handcam: Required for top 10 completions
  • Cheat Indicator: Must be enabled for modded clients (NOT required for vanilla GD)
