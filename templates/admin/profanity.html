How the Filter Works:
  • Normalization: Removes spaces, dots, dashes and applies leetspeak substitutions
  • Severity Levels: Different blocking rules for different contexts
  • Context Aware: Stricter for usernames/levels, more lenient for comments

  • Usernames: Blocks mild, strong, and hate speech
